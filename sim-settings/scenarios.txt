SCENARIO S1: CH PROBABILITY VARIATION (Clustering Density Analysis)
================================================================================
Independent Variable: CH Probability (P)
Fixed Parameters: Nodes=100, Area=500×500m, UAV Speed=10m/s, Energy=0.5J

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
S1-A: Low CH Probability (P = 0.05)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Changed Parameter:
  CH Probability (P)                  : 0.05 (5%)
  Expected CHs per Round              : ~5 CHs

Expected Impact:
  - Fewer CHs → Larger clusters → More intra-cluster transmissions
  - Higher energy consumption per CH (more aggregation)
  - Potentially faster FND for CHs (overloaded)
  - More unclustered nodes (sparse CH coverage)
  - Lower control overhead (fewer ADV broadcasts)
  - UAV may need more waypoints to cover all CHs

OMNeT++ Configuration:
  *.node[*].chProbability = 0.05

Research Questions:
  1. Does FND decrease due to CH overload?
  2. How does unclustered node percentage change?
  3. Impact on PDR and delay with larger clusters?
  4. UAV collection efficiency with fewer targets?
